# Module 1 — Business Requirement

> One-line BR. Day 1 Lab 2 turns this into a structured 5-section prompt.

---

## BR

**We receive a daily ticket-issue file from each airline; we need to validate it, store it for analytics, and flag bad records for ops.**

---

## What's ambiguous (this is the point)

- **"Daily file"** — CSV? JSON? Schema? File size? Arrival window?
- **"Validate"** — against what rules? Schema only, or referential integrity?
- **"Store for analytics"** — Where? S3 zones? Retention? Encryption? Cost ceiling?
- **"Flag bad records"** — Email? Queue? Dashboard? Re-process automatically?

Your Lab 2 task is to **fill in the context** the BR is missing, in your prompt.

---

## Constraints to bake into your prompt

- Files: CSV, ~50 MB per airline, daily arrival.
- Required fields: `pnr`, `airline_code`, `fare_class`, `od_pair`, `settlement_date`.
- Arrival path: S3 raw zone via SFTP gateway.
- Region: ap-south-1.
- Sandbox caps in `.windsurfrules` apply.

## Non-goals (do not propose)

- Glue Streaming.
- Step Functions (we'll get there in Module 5; not for the M1 BR).
- Any GCP / Azure service.
- Multi-region designs.
